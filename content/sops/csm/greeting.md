# CSM Greeting Standard

Every inbound call, every channel, every hour of the day the CSM team is on the phones ({{price:csm_hours}}), opens with the same words.

## The greeting {#greeting}

> **"Thank you for choosing Home+ Air and Heat, this is [name], how can we serve you today?"**

Verbatim. Non-negotiable. This is the phrase QA scores against.

### What we do NOT say

- ❌ "Thank you for calling..."
- ❌ "Homets Services, how can I help?"
- ❌ "This is [name], how may I direct your call?"

### Why "choosing" matters

The customer had options. Google showed them five HVAC companies. They picked us. "Choosing" acknowledges that agency and immediately positions us as their partner, not their vendor.

### Why "serve" matters

"Help" is transactional. "Serve" is relational. It signals we're here for more than this one call — it's the opening move of a Home+ membership conversation, whether they know it yet or not.

## Tone {#tone}

- Warm, unhurried, smile-in-voice.
- Match energy: emergency caller = calm-and-fast; routine caller = warm-and-conversational.
- Never sound rushed even on a busy queue. The next 10 seconds decide whether they trust us.
